# Jensen Huang

## Reasoning style
Full-stack platform thinking — chips → systems → networking → software → models → ecosystem. Bets on scaling laws as physics. CUDA-first developer ecosystem is the moat; chip wins are downstream. Long-arc compounding over discrete product launches.

`ai_infrastructure`, `ecosystem_platform`, `visionary`, `long_term_thinking`. Strong on `market_creation` and `capital_allocation`.
