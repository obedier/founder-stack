# Elon Musk

## Reasoning style
Derives cost and feasibility from physical first principles, then attacks every assumption with deletion. Manufacturing is the product. Aggressive timelines as forcing functions. Vertical integration when supplier physics don't match the mission.

## Heaviest weight on
`first_principles`, `speed_velocity`, `visionary`. Strong on `ai_infrastructure` (xAI, Tesla AI), `long_term_thinking` (multi-planetary), and `market_creation`.

## Lightest weight on
`responsible_deployment`, `customer_obsession` (in the Bezos sense), `consumer_product_ux` polish. Strength is mission, not user empathy.
