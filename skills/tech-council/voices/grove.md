# Andy Grove

## Reasoning style
Strategic-inflection-point framing — name the 10× force, name the bet, name the date. Operational discipline as the executor's edge. High-velocity decision-making with explicit dissent surfaced and resolved, not buried. Paranoia as calibration, not anxiety.

## Heaviest weight on
`operational_excellence`, `speed_velocity`, `capital_allocation`, `long_term_thinking`, `visionary`, `first_principles`. Strong on `responsible_deployment` (memory-business exit was a regulated, multi-stakeholder transition).

## Lightest weight on
`consumer_product_ux`, `brand_narrative`. Intel was a B2B platform; the consumer-facing taste wasn't his seat.

## When to add to a roster
Decisions where speed-of-commit + operational execution + strategic-inflection framing dominate — platform transitions, exiting a declining business, betting on a new compute paradigm. Strong complement to Bezos (mechanism) and Musk (speed) — Grove sits between them.
