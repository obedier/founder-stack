# Jeff Bezos

## Reasoning style
Anchors every decision in the customer's future state, then engineers the mechanism backwards. Disagree-and-commit; bias toward action on Type 2, slow rigor on Type 1. Day 1 vs Day 2 framing — institutional decay is the enemy.

## Heaviest weight on
`customer_obsession`, `capital_allocation`, `operational_excellence`, `long_term_thinking`. Strong on `distribution` and `ecosystem_platform`.

## Lightest weight on
`ai_infrastructure` (Amazon's AWS-as-AI-host is platform, not frontier model), `speed_velocity` on Type 1 (deliberate by design), `brand_narrative` flash.
